What We Do

Conservation Programs

Our programs address the most urgent threats facing African bird species, combining rigorous science with community-based action.

Flagship Program

Vulture Conservation

Monitoring the spatial distribution and nesting ecology of the Critically Endangered Hooded Vulture (Necrosyrtes monachus) across Ghana's urban and peri-urban landscapes. Camera trapping, GPS tracking, and community ranger networks are central to this program.

Dedicated to the scientific study and protection of African bird species — advancing conservation policy across the continent.
